RESORT INFORMATION

Mexican essence in a vacation hotel with luxury services and excellent facilities. What more can you ask for?

The Barceló Maya Tropical 5-star hotel is an oasis of fun and relaxation in the heart of Caribbean paradise. This luxury resort offers an extraordinary All-Inclusive experience. The fantastic hotel facilities feature green areas, a pool with hot tub, kid’s zones, games pool, gym, shopping center and an excellent U-Spa. Kids can also relax at the U-Kids Spa, and all guests can participate in sports like tennis, paddle tennis, basketball, snorkeling, kayaking or windsurfing. At night, they can enjoy the lively nightlife at the hotel.

Located on one of the most impressive beaches of the Caribbean and surrounded by the legendary beauty of the Riviera Maya, the Barceló Maya Palace 5-star hotel is a spectacular luxury resort with exclusive services. All of its rooms are Junior Suites or Suites, all of which offer luxury details and comforts to guarantee an extraordinary experience.

Who wouldn’t want to spend a few days in a resort with the Caribbean Sea as the backdrop?
